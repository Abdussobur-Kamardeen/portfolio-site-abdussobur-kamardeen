About me

My Story

Hi, I`m Abdussobur but you can call me “Abdul”. I`m UX/UI designer with a background in media and customer services. During my time in the media industry, (BT Sport), I gained a lot of experience in pitching, gathering user research to see what viewers would prefer to see. This lead to planning, where I used Excel to create a shot list. I was also involved in the filming process and got to create the logos which were sent to the graphic designers and used in the “Takeover” TV show. This whole process, including the user research, the design process and filming got me interested in the creation of great visual experience for viewers. I`m a problem solver always looking for various solutions to make sure the users are satisfied. This mindset and being an artist, made it natural to transition into UX/UI design career path. I am passionate about creating various products that could possibly be groundbreaking in design world by viewing things from the user`s perspective. I want to be able to create products that impacts a business needs. If you would like to know more, let connect at kamardeenabdussobur@gmail.com or LinkedIn.
